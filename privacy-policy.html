Privacy Policy

Language: English · 简体中文

Effective date: May 11, 2026

Subscription Ledger respects your privacy. The app is designed to minimize data collection and keep your subscription data on your device by default.

1. Data We Collect

Subscription Ledger does not actively collect, upload, or analyze your subscription records.

Subscription names, amounts, billing dates, payment methods, notes, and related information you create in the app are stored locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

The app uses iOS local storage to save your subscription data. Unless you choose to export it, your data does not leave your device.

3. Notifications

If you enable renewal reminders, the app uses iOS local notifications to remind you before a billing date.

Notification content is generated locally on your device and does not require a server.

4. In-App Purchases

Pro purchases are processed by Apple App Store. We do not receive your bank card, payment account, or other payment credentials.

5. Data Export

If you use CSV export, the exported file is created and shared by you. Please keep exported files secure.

6. Third-Party Services

The current version does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, AI services, or our own server.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not designed to collect personal information from children and does not knowingly collect children’s data.

8. Changes to This Policy

If future versions add iCloud sync, crash analytics, or other features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Contact Us

If you have privacy-related questions, contact us at luanlzsn@gmail.com.
